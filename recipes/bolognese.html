Recipe to make bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of tomato passata
  • 1 onion
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1 small carrot
  • 500g of minced meat (mixed pork and beef is the best in my opinion)
  • spices and herbs (basil, oregano)
  1. Put olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, celery, carrot, and 1 pinch salt; cook and stir until onion turns translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir meat into vegetables; cook and stir until meat is crumbly and no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Season with 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, nutmeg, cayenne pepper, and black pepper.
  3. Pour tomato mixture into the pan; fill the tomato can with a bit of water, shake it and stir into sauce. Bring to a boil.
  4. After the meat is soft and tender, mix in some fresh herbs, cook it for 2 more minutes and remove from heat. Serve hot over pasta. Enjoy!
